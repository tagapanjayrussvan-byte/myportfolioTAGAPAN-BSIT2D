About Us
PANABO'S PREMIER
PICKLE HUB

Panabo Pickle Hub is Davao del Norte's first dedicated pickleball facility. We built this platform to make it easier for players at all levels to connect, compete, and grow.

  • 🏓
    4 Premium CourtsCombination of indoor and outdoor courts with professional-grade surfaces and lighting.
  • 📊
    DUPR-Integrated RankingsYour win rate and DUPR rating update automatically after every logged match.
  • ⚡
    Smart Queue SystemOur rotation algorithm ensures fair play and balanced matchmaking for all open play sessions.
  • 🏆
    Regular TournamentsMonthly events across all skill levels — from beginner cups to advanced open championships.
